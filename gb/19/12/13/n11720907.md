<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】花生眉豆火鸡骨粥 养生又暖胃</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/7-7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>花生眉豆火鸡骨粥（图/梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>寒冷的天气，来一碗暖呼呼的热<a href="https://github.com/fxikhp286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A5.md">粥</a>，不仅养生又暖胃。据记载粥有疗病的功效，西汉名医淳于意曾用“火齐粥”为齐王治病。《本草纲目》亦记载不同的粥品有个别的疗效，有治脾胃虚的小麦粥、糯米粥等。</p>
<p>怎么做出一道有广东味的<a href="https://github.com/fxikhp286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A5.md">粥</a>品呢？那么就要注意煮粥的小细节是否到位，有人说：粥的灵魂在于“粥底”的熬煮。掌握好米和水的比例，火侯的时间，高汤的熬制，煮成的粥想必是家常味的餐厅粥品。</p>
<p>梁厨以丰富的经验和独家的食谱，深入浅出的道出粥品的料理过程。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><a href="https://github.com/fxikhp286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E7%94%9F.md">花生</a><a href="https://github.com/fxikhp286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%89%E8%B1%86.md">眉豆</a><a href="https://github.com/fxikhp286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E9%B8%A1.md">火鸡</a>骨粥</span></h3>
<p><strong>【材料】</strong><br />
烤熟<a href="https://github.com/fxikhp286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E9%B8%A1.md">火鸡</a>骨架一个<br />
<a href="https://github.com/fxikhp286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E7%94%9F.md">花生</a>4oz<br />
<a href="https://github.com/fxikhp286/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%89%E8%B1%86.md">眉豆</a>4oz<br />
白米2杯<br />
姜5片<br />
芫茜葱各少许</p>
<p><strong>【做法】</strong><br />
1.花生、眉豆和白米用水浸泡一会儿后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11720925" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1-16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11720925" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/1-16-450x338.jpg" alt="花生,眉豆,火鸡,粥" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">花生、眉豆和白米用水浸泡一会儿。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将烤好的火鸡骨斩开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11720926" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11720926" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2-11-450x338.jpg" alt="花生,眉豆,火鸡,粥" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烤好的火鸡骨斩开。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.火鸡骨放入一个大锅内，加入18碗水、花生、眉豆和姜片，大火煮滚后转为中小火，续炖1小时后捞出骨架，把肉挑出留用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11720927" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/3-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11720927" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/3-8-450x338.jpg" alt="花生,眉豆,火鸡,粥" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.另外加入6杯滚水，再加入白米，大火煮滚，不要盖上锅盖，以免汤汁溢出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11720928" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/4-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11720928" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/4-7-450x338.jpg" alt="花生,眉豆,火鸡,粥" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加水后大火煮滚，要注意不要盖上锅盖，以免汤汁溢出。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.大火煮约30分钟，直到白米绵滑，再将挑出来的鸡肉放回去，多煮5分钟便可上桌，可加入适量的盐、胡椒粉及芫茜葱一起进食，口味更佳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11720934" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/5-6-e1576234596658.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11720934" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/5-6-e1576234596658-450x600.jpg" alt="花生,眉豆,火鸡,粥" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">煮好粥，再加入适量的盐、胡椒粉及芫茜葱一起进食，更加美味。（图/梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
