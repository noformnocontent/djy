<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：海港</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/dc8e41159437f699689a2b43dd28d65b-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图为好美渔港的落日余晖。（蔡上海／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>像终日奔波疲惫不堪的工作者回到了家，船回到了海港。</p>
<p>像浪迹天涯饱受风霜的游子投向慈母的怀抱，船投向了海港。</p>
<p>温暖，安全，没有风浪，海港果真是船的家。</p>
<p>慈祥，和平，没有凶暴，海港果真是慈母的胸怀。</p>
<p>在这里，可以安心地休息，消除疲劳，吸取泥土的气息，重新充实自己，振作自己，准备自己，再度出发。</p>
<p>虽然，工作疲倦了是需要休息的。这样才能消除疲劳，振作精神，再度出发。――一 个甜美充分的休息，是生命的再造，力量的重生。</p>
<p>看到一艘船，载着一群精神饱满的航海者，从海港出发，乘风破浪前进，欣喜何似！</p>
<p>听到一声船笛了。啊，又有一艘船从海港出发了。</p>
<p>海港拥着一片离情。献上无限祝福，给从海港出发的船……。</p>
<p> The HarbourHsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>The ship backs to the harbour, likes a person who backs to his home after the labourer works all day long and whacked to the wide.</p>
<p>The ship steps into the harbour, likes a vagrant drops plunges into mother’s breast after he roams the remotest corner and has been through the vicissitudes of life.</p>
<p>Warmth, safety, no storm, the harbour is the home of the ship.</p>
<p>Benign, peace, no ferocious, the harbour is truly the breast of mother.</p>
<p>Here, one can set his mind at easy, eliminate his fatigued, inhale the breath of the earth, enrich himself again, rouse himself, prepare himself, set out again.</p>
<p>Even though, it’s necessary to take a rest when tired of work. Thus, it can bestir oneself, to set out again.―a full rest is the recreation of life, reborn of power.</p>
<p>It’s joyful, while seeing a ship carries a group of sailor with full spirit set out tobrave the winds and waves</p>
<p>A siren is heard. Oh, a ship sets out again.</p>
<p>A harbour embraces the felling of departure. May devote bless limitlessly to the ship which sets out from the harbour…@</p>
<p>责任编辑：林芳宇</p>
